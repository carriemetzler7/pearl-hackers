About roots

Our Mission

Roots is a website that connects immigrants from Lantinx countries with resources to ensure a smooth transition to the United States while staying connected to their roots. We provide the information of nearby immigrant resources, such as: school listings, medical clinics, outreach centers, and international stores. Our main purpose is to help immigrants get accustomed to their new lives and to have the proper resources that will make them feel at home.

Our Resources

  • Housing: For immigrants, we provide information about shelters and affordable housing in which they can get accustomed to the new country they are in . We also provide hosting families that can house immigrants.
  • Healthcare: From what we know many families come to other countries without ever seeing a proper health care provider, so one of our missions is to provide them with medical resources, in which they feel safe with.
  • Schools: We know that many families immigrate to the United States with young children. Our website provides schools that are nearby that work with children who are not fluent in English and will give their students extra help to succeed
  • Jobs: We provide help with equipping immigrants with job information that measures up to the skills they have
  • Stores/Outreach: We also provide help with finding organications such as churches and clubs in which they can meet other people that are going through the same process
